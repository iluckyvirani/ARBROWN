Business Introduction

Hu-Vet

Hu-Vet is a coined word derived from the combination of “Human” & “Veterinary” (human and industrial animals). AR Brown contributes to improving people's QOL "quality of life" using the latest medical and food safety technology, and in the field of agriculture and livestock, contributes to the efforts to solve the "food waste problem" by utilizing on-farm inspection technology which can be used safely in the field.
