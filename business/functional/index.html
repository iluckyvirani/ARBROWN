Business Introduction

Fine Chemical

Starting from deal of a European company-made Intermediate in 1950, AR Brown serves Pharmaceutical Industry across Asia by supplying Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs), Intermediates and Excipients. Also, for Biomedicine Industry, AR Brown started doing contracted development and manufacturing organization (CDMO) coordination business such as Antibody Drug Conjugate (ADC) In addition, High purity chemicals are supplied to Fine Chemical industry for Electronics applications.
