CSR Activities

AR Brown group, which has accumulated a history of more than 130 years since the foundation of A.R Brown McFarlane in Glasgow, U.K by Captain Brown, and more than 70 years since his grandson Albert Pine Brown founded the Tokyo branch, has the basis build from the great aspiration (adventurer spirit) that the two Browns had, namely to "Take the lead for the times and contribute to new value creation."

As members of the AR Brown Group, we are proud of the aspiration, which we have set as our Management Philosophy, and it is our responsibility to contribute to the realization of a prosperous society through trading activities to contribute to advanced technology development around the world. Furthermore, while the business globalization is progressing, we should be responsible for all potential impact given from the activities to the globe, thus, we make sure of transparency of the Management responding to all the stakeholders in fair, timely and appropriate manners.

"AR Brown Group's CSR" is based on our management philosophy and represents the justice that we must observe and the policies and codes of conduct which should be adhered to act as good corporate citizens in modern society. Each of us takes pride in working for the AR Brown Group which fulfills its social responsibilities by practicing the CSR policies and norms we have set as our management policy so that the AR Brown Group can continue to exist as needed by society for the future.
