AR BROWN (Shanghai) Co., Ltd.

The entity was founded and started operations in December 2011 in Shanghai, China where Electronics Materials, Performance Chemical, Fine Chemical and Test & Measurement businesses are conducted for the China markets. For Performance Chemical and Fine Chemical businesses, it also has functionality to source products exporting to the other entities of the group. Branch office exists in Guangzhou.

AR BROWN (THAILAND) Co., Ltd.

The entity was established in June 2014 in Bangkok where Electronics Materials, Performance Chemical, Fine Chemical, Life Science and Test & Measurement businesses are conducted for the ASEAN markets. For Electronics Materials and Performance Chemical businesses, it also has functionality to source products exporting to the other entities of the group. Salesforces were deployed in Singapore in 2020 and in Vietnam in 2022 respectively.

AR BROWN India Trading Private Limited

The entity was established in April 2018 in Gurgaon, India, the entity started from sourcing of products for Performance Chemical and Fine Chemical businesses. As of now the entity conducts Electronics Materials, Performance Chemical, Fine Chemical, Hu-Vet and Test & Measurement businesses for the India markets. Furthermore, through investment into an Indian API and Intermediate manufacturer, contracted production capability for relevant products has been obtained.
